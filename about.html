THE TEAM

At Holy Angel University (HAU), the Volleyball Team stands as a symbol of athleticism, teamwork, and school pride. Established to provide students with opportunities to excel both academically and athletically, the HAU Volleyball Team has grown to become one of the most competitive and respected collegiate teams in the region.

MISSION

The HAU Volleyball Team strives for excellence in sports and personal growth, guided by Christ-centeredness, integrity, and community. We aim to develop skilled athletes who embody teamwork and responsibility, representing Holy Angel University with pride.

VISION

We envision the HAU Volleyball Team as a leader in collegiate sports, known for both athletic success and strong values. Our athletes will excel on the court while contributing positively to the community and fulfilling their societal responsibilities.

WHO CAN JOIN US?

The HAU Volleyball Program is open to:

  • College or enrolled upper-class high school students at all skill levels, from beginners to advanced players.
  • Athletes striving to compete at a high level or improve their general volleyball skills.
  • Individuals committed to balancing athletics with academics.

OUR PROGRAM STRUCTURE

  • Training Sessions: Held multiple times per week, focusing on drills, technique refinement, and scrimmage play.
  • Coaching Staff: Experienced and certified coaches helping players achieve peak performance.
  • Strength & Conditioning: Regimens designed to improve agility, strength, and endurance specific to volleyball
  • Matches & Tournaments: Local, regional, and national volleyball competitions for committed players.
